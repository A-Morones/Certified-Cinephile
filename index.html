Certified Cinephile

The RottenTomatoes Score Guessing Game!

  • Players will be asked to guess the RottenTomatoes score for a series of movies.
  • Each player will enter their guesses, and the game will compare the guesses to the actual scores.
  • The player whose guess is closest to the actual score wins the round.
  • At the end of the game, the player with the most accurate guesses overall is declared the winner.
  • Have fun and good luck!
